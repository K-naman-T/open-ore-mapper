Open Ore Mapper

Map exposed surface mineral signatures from hyperspectral raster cubes using Spectral Angle Mapper and NNLS abundance estimation.

Important: This tool identifies spectral similarity at or near the surface. A match means the input spectrum is consistent with a reference mineral spectrum. It does not confirm the presence of a buried ore body. Always validate with field sampling, petrology, and geochemistry.
